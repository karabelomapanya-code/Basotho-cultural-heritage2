Music and Dance

On my perspective,I can say the traditional Basotho music is famo.This music features the accordion and an oil-can drum. One of the famo musicians I know is Moketa a.k.a "Kholumo-lumo ea Leribe". This type of music has different genres under it like "Seakhi"e.t.c

When it comes to dance, there's a lot to talk about because of how wide and different it is. I can say dancing is divided by the sexes: men's dances and women's dances. I've never seen traditional dances that are mixed despite if it is in the game like "selia-lia".This dances normally happen during some community ceremonies like: marriages, Christmas,e.t.c. One of the men's dances is called "Mohobelo"which features stamping of feet and one for women's dances is called "Mokhibo" whereby women kneel and move the shoulders and arms.

Traditional dances and Instruments

1. Mohobelo dance

This is a male dance that features the stamping of feet and rasing if hands and sticks

2. Mokhibo dance

This is a female dance whereby women kneel down and move their shoulders and arms

3. Ndlamo dance

This is a male dance that also features the stamping of feet but this time whith a quick rythmn and mostly done by boys

4. Litolobonya dance

This is where women or girls wear huge dresses made from flour bags and has lits if beer bottle.Mostly white

5, Famo dance

This is the type of dance done by men and boys. They hold sticks and shake one shoulder

6. Selia-lia dance

It is a dance done by both boys and girls while chosing their loved once but in a form of a game

7. Liphotha

This is a dance done by a group of boys during ceremonies mentioned earlier.They wear the rubber rain boots and then tap with toes and hit the boots with the same rythmn to make a nice sound

8. Lipina tsa mokopu

This is a dance done by girls wearing "thethana"and they dance running in a circle

9. Mokorotlo dance

This is a dance done by a group of adult men that features a slow foot-stamping and swinging in a same rythmn. This dance is only done for a Chief on special occasions like meeting and inspections. The leader sings with a high-pitched voice

10. Maqekha dance

This is a dance done by women during their initiation while wearing goat skin dresses

11. Lesiba instrument

This instrument consist of a horse hair stretched along a stick between a quill and a holding bracket.Light sucking against the quill causes the horse-hair vibrate and produce a hauting sound

12. Thomo instrument

This instrument consists of a bow, across which a horse-hair or thin wire is stretched, and which is tautened with a wire fastening in the middle.This bow is attached to an oil tin.

13. Lenaka instrument

This instrument is made from an animal horn. It has a small hole at one end and a huge one at the other end. It need heavy breathing to play, its more like a trumpet

14. Khabulu-bulu instrument

This is an instrument made from a bone and has a string at one end, it plays when it spinned in the wind

15. Moritlhoane instrument

This is made from a small horn and has a thin hole through

16. Lekhitlane instrument

This instrument is made from a bow stick and horse-hair or wire and a short stick is used to make a sweet sound

17. Setolo-tolo instrument

This instrument is made with wires and produces different sound according to thee thickness of the wires

18. Sekupu instrument

This instrument is made from animal that covers the opening of an oil tin-can

19. Moropa instrument

This instrument is made from an oil tin-can and has a tyre covering the whole and has some shakers made from beer bottle tops

20. Lekolulo instrument

This instrument is made from a long maize stalk, it has holes on it.It is played by heardboys.All the parts of the stalk should be equal

21. Sekebeku sa lehlaka

This instrument is made from maize stalk but the short one.The other part should be smaller than the other for sound trimming

22. Sekebeku sa ts'epe

This one is made from a metal.It is round on the other side and straight the other side. It has a thin one in between and it extends on the outside.It sounds like a guitar

23. 'Mamokhorong instrument

This is an instrumet made from a long stick, a long wire and a tin-can.It is played using a short bow stick that has a fastened horse-hair.It produces a sound as it moves up and down along the wire
